How it works

Scan. Score. Swap.

Three steps from "what is this?" to a better basket - built for real grocery aisles, not lab conditions.

01

Scan anything

Point at any barcode. No barcode or no match? Photograph the nutrition label - Nutovia reads English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Hindi and more, automatically.

02
💯

Get two honest scores

The product's own 0-100 score - built on the official Nutri-Score 2023 model with ultra-processing and additive adjustments - beside your family's average, where each member's goals and allergies count. Every point explained - never a bare number.

03
🔁

Swap smarter

Below your healthy threshold? Nutovia ranks better products in the same category - starting with what's confirmed at the stores you shop - and one tap opens them in the store's own app.

Radical transparency

You always see where the data came from.

Every scan replays its search as a live flowchart. If a product is missing everywhere, AI researches the web - and those results are clearly marked and held to a lower level of trust, with the sources to check.

No black boxes.

Most food apps show you a grade and ask you to trust it. Nutovia shows its work: which database answered, what was missing, and why the score is what it is. When data is unverified, the app says so - because your family's food choices deserve honesty, not confidence theater.

Built for families

One scan, a verdict for everyone.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

A score per person

Diets, health goals, and allergies for each family member. One scan shows the product score, the family average, and exactly who should skip it.

🚫

Allergens are hard stops

An allergen match doesn't lower the score - it zeroes it, with a clear warning. When in doubt, Nutovia leaves it out.

🌍

Any label, any language

On-device reading for Latin, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, and Hindi scripts - AI interprets the rest. No settings needed.

🏪

Your stores, not "a store"

Pick every chain you shop - from Walmart and Kroger to Amazon and Instacart. Alternatives are matched against all of them.

📚

History that works for you

Every scan saved per family member, searchable and filterable by score. Swipe to remove - your data, your call.

🔒

Private by design

Your household's data stays in your household. No ads, no trackers, no data sales. Export everything anytime.
