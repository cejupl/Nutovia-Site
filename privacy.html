Nutovia Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Nutovia is a free food-scanner app that helps families understand what is in packaged food. This policy explains what data the app handles, why, and the choices you have. The short version: your data belongs to your household, we show no ads, and we never sell data.

Information we collect

  • Account: your email address (and name, if your sign-in provider shares it) when you sign in with Apple, Google, Facebook, or email.
  • Family profiles: the names or nicknames, dietary preferences, health goals, and allergen restrictions you enter for members of your household. This can include sensitive information (for example, allergies); it is used only to personalize scores and suggestions and is visible only to your household.
  • Scan history: the products you scan, their scores, and when you scanned them - stored so you can revisit them in the History tab.
  • Store selections: the store chains you choose, used to rank alternatives.

Camera, photos, and location

  • Camera is used to read barcodes and photograph nutrition labels. Label photos are processed on your device; the extracted text may be sent to our servers for interpretation. Photos themselves are not uploaded or stored by Nutovia.
  • Location is used only when you tap "Detect the store I'm in", only while the app is in use, and only to match a nearby store name. Your location is not stored and is never linked to your identity.

How we use AI

When a product or alternative is not in our databases, Nutovia may send the product's barcode, name, category, label text, and - for personalized suggestions - the dietary preferences of the active profile to an AI service (Anthropic's Claude) to research publicly available nutrition information. No names, email addresses, or account identifiers are included in these requests. AI-sourced results are clearly marked in the app.

Where your data lives

Nutovia's backend runs on Supabase (database, authentication, and secure functions). Access controls ensure each household can only read and write its own data. Product information you help identify (for example, a confirmed label) may be added, without any personal information, to the shared product catalog that benefits all users.

What we don't do

  • No advertising, and no advertising trackers.
  • No selling or renting of personal data, ever.
  • No analytics profiles linked to your identity.

Your choices

  • Export: Settings → "Export my data" gives you a complete copy of your household's data at any time.
  • Correction: profiles and preferences can be edited or removed in the app at any time.

Data deletion

To delete your account and all household data, email admin@nutovia.app from the email address associated with your account with the subject "Delete my Nutovia account". We will delete your account, profiles, and scan history within 30 days and confirm by reply. Anonymous contributions to the shared product catalog (nutrition facts with no personal information) may be retained.

Children

Nutovia accounts are created by adults. Family profiles for children are entered and controlled entirely by the account holder.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will note it in the app and update the date above.
